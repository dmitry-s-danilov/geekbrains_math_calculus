\section*{Задача 1}

Представить в виде несократимой рациональной дроби:

\begin{enumerate}
  \item \( 0.(216 )\)
  \item \( 1.0(01 )\)
\end{enumerate}

\subsection*{Решение}

\subsubsection*{1}

\[ x = 0.(216) \]
\[ 10^3 x = 10^3\ 0.(216) \]
\[ 10^3 x = 216.(216) \]
\[ 10^3 x = 216 + 0.(216) \]
\[ 10^3 x = 216 + x \]
\[ 999 x = 216 \]
\[ x = \frac{216}{999} = \frac{24}{111} \]

\subsubsection*{2}

\[ x = 1.0(01) \]
\[ 10 x = 10\ 1.0(01) \]
\[ 10 x = 10.(01) \]
\[ 10 x = 10 + 0.(01) \]
\[ 10 x = 10 + y \]
\[ x = \frac{10 + y}{10} = 1 + \frac{y}{10} \]

\[ y = 0.(01) \]
\[ 10^2 y = 10^2\ 0.(01) \]
\[ 10^2 y = 1.(01) \]
\[ 10^2 y = 1 + 0.(01) \]
\[ 10^2 y = 1 + y \]
\[ 99 y = 1 \]
\[ y = \frac{1}{99} \]

\[ x = 1 + \frac{y}{10} = 1 + \frac{1}{990} \]

\subsection*{Ответ}

\begin{enumerate}
  \item \( 0.(216) = \frac{24}{111} \)
  \item \( 1.0(01) = 1 + \frac{1}{990} \)
\end{enumerate}
